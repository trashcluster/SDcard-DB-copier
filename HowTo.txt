SD card databases updater

1 - Generate and download the latest database from stripesapp
2 - unzip the file
3 - Crzate a folder named 'MatchDatabases' and put the database in it
4 - launch omsdb.exe
5 - select the MatchDatabases folder on your pc
6 - Select a working mode (Database updater : wipe the sdcard and put the new database on it	Cleaner : erase the 2 temporary .sqlite files from the sdcard but keep the current database)
7 - Plug the sdcard and wait for "Done ! You can safely remove the sdcard" to appear on the screen
8 - unplug the sdcard
9 - repeat from 7
10 - enjoy
